Full checklist before hitting upload:

EN pin of programmer board shorted to GND
Programmer RX → target TX GPIO 1
Programmer TX → target RX GPIO 3
Common GND between both boards
Target ESP32 powered (3.3V from programmer board's 3.3V pin or your own supply)
Target GPIO 0 pulled LOW (BOOT button held or jumpered to GND)
Target EN briefly pulled LOW then released (RESET) to enter flash mode
Hit upload in Arduino IDE
